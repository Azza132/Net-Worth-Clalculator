
# Net Worth Calculator

A simple, offline net worth tracking tool that runs entirely in your browser.
No accounts. No internet required. All data is saved locally on your device.

---

## Overview

This site allows you to:

* Track your assets
* Track your liabilities
* Automatically calculate your net worth
* Save monthly snapshots
* View your net worth trend over time
* See your asset allocation breakdown
* Store everything locally in your browser

Your net worth is calculated as:

Net Worth = Total Assets − Total Liabilities

---

## How to Use

### 1. Enter Your Assets

On the left side, you will see the Assets section.

Assets are grouped into categories:

* Cash & Savings
* Investments
* Property
* Other Assets

For each item:

* Enter a name (e.g., “Current Account”)
* Enter the value
* Click the + Add button to add more items
* Click × to delete an item

Totals update automatically.

---

### 2. Enter Your Liabilities

On the right side, you will see the Liabilities section.

Liabilities are grouped into:

* Mortgage
* Loans & Credit
* Other Debts

Add your debts the same way as assets:

* Enter name
* Enter value
* Use + Add to create new entries
* Use × to remove items

Totals update instantly.

---

### 3. View Your Net Worth

At the top of the page, you will see:

* Total Net Worth
* Total Assets
* Total Liabilities

If your net worth is:

* Positive → it appears in green
* Negative → it appears in red

All numbers update automatically as you type.

---

### 4. Save a Monthly Snapshot

To track your progress over time:

1. Select a month using the date picker.
2. Click “SAVE THIS MONTH”.

This stores:

* Total assets
* Total liabilities
* Net worth

If you already saved a snapshot for that month, you can overwrite it.

---

### 5. View Your Net Worth Chart

The “Net Worth Over Time” chart shows:

* Net worth (solid green line)
* Assets (dashed green line)
* Liabilities (dashed red line)

You must save at least two monthly snapshots to see the trend line.

---

### 6. View Snapshot History

The Snapshot History table shows:

* Month
* Total assets
* Total liabilities
* Net worth
* Change from previous month

You can:

* Delete individual snapshots using ×
* Clear all history using the “CLEAR HISTORY” button

Warning: Clearing history cannot be undone.

---

### 7. Asset Allocation

The donut chart shows how your assets are distributed.

It displays:

* Each asset’s percentage of total assets
* The monetary value
* A color-coded breakdown

Only assets with a value greater than zero appear.

---

## Data Storage & Privacy

* All data is saved using your browser’s local storage.
* Nothing is sent to a server.
* No account required.
* No internet required.
* If you clear your browser data, your saved information will be lost.

For best safety, avoid using private/incognito mode.

---

## Tips for Best Use

* Update values monthly before saving a snapshot.
* Keep item names consistent to track properly.
* Save snapshots at the same time each month.
* Review the chart to monitor long-term trends.
* Use the allocation chart to identify overexposure in assets.

---
